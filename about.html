About Evaly

Built to help students
evaluate themselves better.

Evaly is an online learning platform where students discover courses, take secure quizzes, and get instant feedback — all in one place.

What Evaly Offers

Everything a student or teacher needs, in one platform.

📚

Curated Courses

Browse featured courses across Math, Science, and Technology — structured for beginner to intermediate learners.

✍️

Secure Quizzes

Take timed, structured quizzes with instant results. Designed to test real understanding, not just memorization.

⚡

Instant Feedback

Get your results immediately after every quiz — so you know exactly where you stand and what to improve.

🏫

Teacher Tools

Teachers can create and manage quizzes, track student progress, and share content with their classes easily.

🏆

Leaderboard

Friendly competition keeps students motivated. See how you rank among peers and push yourself to improve.

📬

Direct Contact

Have a question or feedback? Reach the Evaly team directly through the built-in contact form on the homepage.

How It Works

Three simple steps to get started.

1

Browse & Pick a Course

Explore the course catalogue and pick a subject that interests you — from algebra to Python programming.

2

Take a Quiz

After going through the material, take a quiz to test what you've learned. Questions are timed and structured fairly.

3

Get Your Results Instantly

Your score appears the moment you finish. Review what you got wrong and come back to improve your ranking.
